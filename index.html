A LOCATION EQUALLY DISTANT...

In Equidistant, you live an ordinary life, going to work, school, hanging out with your friends. However, after a tragic accident you find yourself in limbo, the land between the living and dead. Whilst navigating this eternal realm, you wish to return home to your family and friends. You must embark on a journey to meet a fabled wizard, one who can grant you your old life back. As you traverse this realm, you discover your affinity for magic, fight fierce creatures, befriend new faces, and make choices altering your adventure. These choices will not only affect you, but the fate of those around you. At the end of your journey, you must make a pivotal decision.

ABOUT THIS GAME

This is a choose your own adventure game, ultimately leading to meeting the wizard who can grant your old life back. Decisions you make will impact your abilities with magic, overall skill, and friendships. Therefore, you can curate your own experience and cater to the abilities you like the most. The story puts your morals to the test when making decisions such as staying in limbo or returning to the land of the living. The game being set in a limbo environment creates a mixture of familiar “human” settings with fantastical elements, keeping you on your toes. The challenges you encounter lead to character growth and rewards enhancing your experience. When making friends or enemies, you can expand those relationships through trust, betrayal, romance, trading spells, bribing and much more. The puzzles and mysteries found in this game offer challenging yet fun ways to advance through the game and obtain powerful items.

KEY FEATURES

  • Exploration of the vast limbo realm, uncovering its secrets
  • Customize the look of your character as they journey and obtain new garbs and trinkets
  • Meet and expand your relationships with friends and enemies
  • Solve intricate puzzles in exchange for magical rewards or tricks
  • Craft spells and potions to trade, use, and upgrade
